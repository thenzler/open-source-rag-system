ACME Corporation - Employee Policy Manual

REMOTE WORK POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. ELIGIBILITY
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (6 months) are eligible for remote work arrangements. Part-time employees may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

2. WORK SCHEDULE
Remote workers must maintain core hours between 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM in their local time zone. Flexible hours are permitted outside of core hours with manager approval.

3. EQUIPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY
- Company will provide laptop, monitor, and necessary software
- Employees must have reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Home office setup must meet ergonomic standards
- IT support available during business hours

4. COMMUNICATION REQUIREMENTS
- Daily check-ins with immediate supervisor
- Weekly team meetings via video conference
- Response time: Email within 4 hours, Slack within 1 hour during business hours
- Quarterly in-person meetings required

5. PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS
Performance will be measured by results, not hours worked. Key metrics include:
- Project completion rates
- Quality of deliverables
- Team collaboration effectiveness
- Customer satisfaction scores

6. SECURITY PROTOCOLS
- VPN connection required for all work
- Company data must not be stored on personal devices
- Regular security training mandatory
- Report security incidents immediately

7. TERMINATION OF REMOTE WORK
Remote work privileges may be revoked for:
- Consistent performance issues
- Security violations
- Failure to meet communication requirements
- Business needs requiring on-site presence
